# Why GitHub Blog Should Be the Primary Technical Blog

The main technical archive should live where the brand lives. That is the core reason to promote the GitHub-backed site blog into the primary publishing surface.

## Advantages

- One canonical archive under the portfolio domain
- Stronger internal linking from projects to articles
- More durable positioning than spreading core writing across platforms
- Better control over article structure, sequencing, and curation

## Role of external channels

External channels still matter, but as distribution and audience-fit surfaces. They should amplify the core archive, not replace it.
